Nuestro Equipo de expertos

Alexander Sotelo

Alex is responsible for the management of all financial transactions at Open English, as well as corporate financial planning, tax and audit. He is a licensed CPA with more than 15 years of financial management experience in roles at leading multinational digital media and broadcasting companies. Having worked extensively throughout the globe, Alex has focused his efforts during the last decade helping run businesses operating in Latin America. Prior to joining Open English, Alex held the position of Latin America Controller at Yahoo! and previously held financial management positions at Discovery Communications and CBS/Viacom. He began his career working for KPMG, LLP. Alex is a cum laude graduate of Florida International University’s School of Business where he obtained a B.S. in Accounting, and obtained an MBA in International Business from the Keller Graduate School of Business. Alex, the son of Cuban immigrants, speaks fluent Spanish, advanced Portuguese and is conversational in Italian.

Wilmer Sarmiento

Wilmer first teamed up with Andres Moreno while the two were engineering students at the Universidad Simon Bolivar (USB) in Caracas, Venezuela. As the founding VP of Technology, Wilmer became the chief architect for the early online learning platform and was heading up infrastructure research and framework validation – his specialty. From there he moved onto leading the Payment Systems division. Wilmer has a degree in Computer Engineering from USB, having achieved the highest GPA in the history of the department. Without a day of traditional classroom training, Wilmer has also taught himself English, largely through the development of the Online School of Open English.

John C. McIntire

John is a retired Partner of Goldman, Sachs & Co., where he spent 12 years and was CEO of the Latin American region. He has 30 years of experience in capital markets and banking, primarily in Latin America, and is currently an advisor to and director of a New York-based fund focused on that region. John is a member of the Global Advisory Board of Endeavor, the leading non-profit focused on entrepreneurship in the emerging markets, the Board of the Cuba Study Group and the Council of the Americas. Cuban-born, John holds a B.A. in Economics from Princeton. He resides in the New York City area.

Jon Karlen

Jon is a General Partner at Flybridge Capital Partners, whose investment interests and experience include enterprise software, consumer-focused products and online services, and energy technology. Jon currently represents the firm on the boards of Digital Lumens, Open English, and Virtual Computer, Inc. He also previously sat on the boards of Eka Systems (acquired by Cooper Power Systems), GamerDNA (acquired by Crispy Gamer), and ZING Systems (acquired by Dell). Prior to joining Flybridge, Jon served as Director of Product Marketing at OATSystems, an RFID software company. He holds a B.A. in Philosophy from Harvard University and an MBA from Stanford University Graduate School of Business.
